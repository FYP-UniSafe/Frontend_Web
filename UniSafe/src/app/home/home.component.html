What is UniSafe?

Empowering Voices, Fighting GBV. It's what we do.

UniSafe is a comprehensive platform designed to address gender-based violence (GBV) and related issues within universities. Our mission
is to empower students to speak out, report incidents, access resources, and foster
a safer and more equitable campus environment.

UniSafe can help you to:

Report GBV incidents

Report for youself or your friend,
be able to attach evidence and
get the justice you deserve

Get psychological help

Get counselling from our professional counselors willing to provide you with the best legal advice

Join Gender community

Join a supportive community dedicated to gender equality. Engage with fellow advocates for gender justice.
